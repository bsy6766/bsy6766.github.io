ABOUT



Hello world. My name is Seung Youp Baek.



Everything started when I played Super Mario World for the first time. Since then, I wanted to create my own video game in the future, and the desire to become a game developer made myself motivated and it's still ongoing.



Although I have focused my career path as a game developer since the college, I am also interested in many other fields in software engineering. Majoring Computer Science introduced myself to tons of different fields other than game development. I am always opened mind and passionate about software engineering.



When it comes to working on my projects and do daily tasks, whether it's concept or programming, I iterate through my own process. Instead of jumping in right away, I break down the task to smaller tasks, research and design, iterate trials & errors, and finish as my own implementation.
